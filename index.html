What is Reverse Polish Notation?

Reverse Polish Notation, also known as postfix or RPN notation, in a mathematical notation in which operators follow their operands.

How does RPN work?

Converting infix notation to RPN is as simple as moving the operator to the right of the two operands!

RPN Infix Result
4 3 + 4+3 7
3 2 ^ π x (3^2)xπ 28.27
1 3 + 12 4 + ÷ (1+3)/(12+4) 0.25
4 2 5 3 - + ÷ 4/(2+(5-3)) 1

How to use this calculator

To use this calculator, press the digit buttons, followed by 'Enter' to input a number. After inputing your operators, press 'Enter' to submit the expression. You can use the ∧ and ∨ buttons to view input history, and C to reset the current display.
